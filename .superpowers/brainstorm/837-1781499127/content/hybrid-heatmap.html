B — Linked Views
Strip overview on top (all indicators at once). Clicking a dot filters the ranked table below to that indicator's full country list. Two components, one data story.
Tradeoff: A (Strip-in-Table) — single unified component, scannable at a glance, fewer interactions needed. Recommended if the page is already busy. B (Linked Views) — richer interaction, strip gives spatial overview then table gives full detail on demand. Better for power users who want to investigate one indicator deeply.
